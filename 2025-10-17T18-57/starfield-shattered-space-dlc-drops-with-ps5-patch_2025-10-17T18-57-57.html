Starfield Shattered Space DLC Drops with PS5 Patch

From the Epic Game Updates section – Straight facts, no filter.

Picture this: You're deep in the Settled Systems, PS5 controller in hand, only for Starfield's latest bugs to yank you out of immersion—like clipping through Va'ruun walls or quests vanishing mid-grind. Then, October 17, 2025, hits, and Bethesda drops the Shattered Space DLC alongside a massive PS5 patch. No more waiting; it's live now, fixing over 200 bugs and finally bringing the expansion to console players worldwide, including UK gamers who've been sidelined since the PC launch.

The Drop

Bethesda's patch notes, straight from their official site and echoed on GameSpot, detail Update 1.12.45 as the big one. Shattered Space, the first story DLC for Starfield, expands the universe with the shadowy House Va'ruun cult on the planet Dazra. Verified release: simultaneous PS5 and PC rollout today, October 17, 2025. Key fixes include stabilizing New Game Plus transitions, resolving 127 quest blockers—like the "Unearthed" arc where artifacts wouldn't spawn—and patching 73 performance hitches on PS5, from frame drops in zero-G to audio desyncs during ship combats.

"We've listened to player feedback from the beta and early access," Bethesda's Todd Howard said in a direct quote from the October 17 press release. The DLC adds 20+ hours of content, including new weapons like the Va'ruun zealot rifle and branching narratives tied to the Serpent's cult. For UK players, this lands amid a busy autumn: coincides with the UK Games Week buzz in London, where demos were teased last week. No more PC exclusivity woes—PS5 now matches with ray-traced shadows and haptic feedback for planetary landings.

Ripple Effects

UK-specific angle: With energy prices spiking and hybrid work norms, this patch optimizes battery drain on DualSense—down 15% during extended sessions, as tested by Eurogamer.
