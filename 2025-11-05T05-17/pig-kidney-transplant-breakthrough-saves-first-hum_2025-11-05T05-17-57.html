From Lab to Life-Saver

Back in 2024, surgeons transplanted a pig kidney into a human for the first time, and the patient survived longer than ever before. Fast-forward to now: the first official clinical trial started on November 4, testing these kidneys in more patients at places like Massachusetts General Hospital.
