RTX 3060 Locks in the Top Spot

The survey, based on millions of Steam users worldwide but spotlighting UK trends, shows the RTX 3060 at 8.9% usage—edging out rivals like the RTX 4060 at 7.2%. This mid-range beast from 2020 refuses to fade, delivering ray-traced graphics and DLSS tech that boosts frames in demanding titles. For UK players grinding daily sessions, it means consistent play on setups under £800, perfect for school-night raids. No major shifts from October's data, where it reclaimed the lead after a brief dip—proving its staying power in a market flooded with newer cards.
