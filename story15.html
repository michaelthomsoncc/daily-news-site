Doors open at 6:30 PM tonight, October 17, 2025, with support act DJ Nova warming up the crowd. Local fans buzz about the show's energy—expect pyrotechnics and interactive sing-alongs. Newcastle's music scene thrives here; it's hosted over 2,000 events since launch, drawing North East youth like us.
