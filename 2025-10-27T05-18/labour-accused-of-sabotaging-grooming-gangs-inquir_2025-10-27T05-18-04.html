The Grooming Gangs Probe Basics

Grooming gangs have haunted UK towns for years, targeting kids from all backgrounds with manipulation and abuse. Reports from places like Rotherham exposed how thousands suffered while authorities fumbled. Fast-forward to 2025: a national inquiry kicked off under the last government to dig into failures and prevent repeats. But since Labour took power in July 2024, progress has hit snags. The October 27 Conservative Home update highlights fresh accusations that the government is slow-walking key steps, like appointing a chair or setting timelines. Victims' groups say this leaves families in limbo, waiting for answers that could shield today's kids from similar traps.

Badenoch Fires Back Hard
