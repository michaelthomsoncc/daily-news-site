Episode eight: light and sound. Rapid fire.

Specular reflection: smooth surface, clear image. Diffuse reflection: rough surface, scatters light.

Converging lens thicker in middle — can form real or virtual images depending on object distance. Diverging lens always gives virtual, upright, diminished image.

Ray diagrams: parallel ray through principal focus after lens; ray through centre undeviated.

Critical angle: light from denser to less dense medium — total internal reflection when angle exceeds critical angle. Used in optical fibres.

Sound needs a medium — cannot travel in vacuum. Pitch linked to frequency. Loudness linked to amplitude.

Human hearing roughly twenty hertz to twenty kilohertz. Ultrasound above twenty kilohertz — sonar, foetal scanning, cleaning.

Echo: reflected sound. Distance equals speed times time divided by two.

Colour: objects absorb some wavelengths, reflect others. White reflects all; black absorbs most.

Red shift: light from distant galaxies shifted to longer wavelengths — suggests universe expansion.
