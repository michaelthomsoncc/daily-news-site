Episode ten: space physics and mixed recall. Rapid fire.

Solar system: Sun at centre. Planets orbit due to gravitational force providing centripetal acceleration. Orbits are elliptical.

Geostationary orbit: twenty-four hour period, above equator, used for communications satellites. Low Earth orbit: lower altitude, shorter period, used for imaging and ISS.

Weightlessness in orbit: astronaut and spacecraft fall together — continuous free fall around Earth.

Life cycle of star: nebula to main sequence; small stars end as white dwarf; massive stars become red supergiant, supernova, then neutron star or black hole.

Red shift of distant galaxies supports expanding universe. Big Bang theory models origin.

Quick mixed recall: Ohm's law V equals I R. Efficiency useful over total. Half-life halves activity. F equals m a. Density m over V. Alpha stopped by paper. Gamma needs thick lead.
