Arrow Lake Unveiled: Faster Chips for Desktop Power

Intel dropped the Core Ultra 200S Arrow Lake lineup as the official successor to Lunar Lake. These desktop CPUs target gamers and creators with serious upgrades. The series includes five models: the entry-level Core Ultra 5 245K and 245KF, plus beefier Core Ultra 7 265K and 265KF options. Faster clock speeds mean quicker load times and smoother gameplay, while integrated NPUs handle AI tasks without draining your power bill. In CES follow-up builds tested on October 25, these chips powered compact rigs that stayed cool under heavy gaming loads, ideal for young players stacking hours in battle royales.
