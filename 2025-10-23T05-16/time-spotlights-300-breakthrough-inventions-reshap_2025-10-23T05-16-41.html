ESR's Tech That Makes Life Smarter

ESR, a global tech accessories brand, snagged spots on TIME's list for three inventions redefining charging, working, and traveling. Their HaloLock Open Earbuds deliver wireless audio without cords getting in the way, perfect for UK gamers dodging tangles during Roblox marathons. The Shift Magnetic Keyboard snaps onto iPads for instant laptop vibes, turning study sessions into pro setups amid school pressures. And the Bounce Magnetic Power Bank sticks to your phone for on-the-go charging, keeping your battle royale alive without outlets. "ESR has been recognized in TIME’s Best Inventions of 2025 list across various categories, highlighting innovations that make charging, working, and traveling easier and smarter," the announcement stated. For a 12-year-old in Manchester or London, this means less downtime—charge while commuting on the Tube or powering up during family trips, directly hitting the daily hustle of school and play.

iFIT's Treadmill Turns Exercise into Adventure

In the Design category, iFIT's NordicTrack Ultra 1 treadmill earned a shoutout for blending luxury smarts with fitness. This machine syncs with interactive workouts, making runs feel like epic quests—imagine jogging through virtual worlds while leveling up your stamina for real-life PE or after-school sports. "iFIT, Inc., a global leader in connected fitness and interactive content, today announced its inclusion on TIME’s annual Best Inventions of 2025 list in the Design category," per the October 22 release. It features adaptive inclines and speeds that match your pace, cutting boredom and boosting health. UK kids facing rainy weather or screen-time limits will dig how it gamifies movement, tying into wellness trends amid global pushes for active lifestyles. No more staring at walls; it's play disguised as exercise, impacting energy levels for gaming marathons later.
