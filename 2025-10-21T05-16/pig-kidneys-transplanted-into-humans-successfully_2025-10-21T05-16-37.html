From Labs to Real Patients

Dig into the deets: these pig organs get CRISPR-edited to remove viruses and human-repelling proteins. A landmark? That kidney transplant in March 2024 at Massachusetts General Hospital, where a 62-year-old got a genetically modified pig kidney and breathed easier for two months post-op.
