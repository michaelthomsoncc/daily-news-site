On October 20, 2025, fresh news dropped about this non-invasive headset that's changing lives for people who can't speak, like those with ALS or locked-in syndrome. What is Cognixion's Device?

Cognixion's invention, spotlighted in TIME's Best Inventions of 2025 list via a Morningstar Accesswire release on October 10, 2025, is a lightweight headset that sits on your head without surgery. It uses EEG sensors to pick up electrical signals from your brain. The AI then decodes those signals to predict what you're trying to say or do. For someone unable to speak, it's like having a superpower: think "I want water," and it speaks it out loud or types it on a screen. Recent updates confirm it's already in trials, helping users communicate at speeds up to 90 words per minute—faster than some texting sessions during a quick match.
