The Tech That Changes Everything

Samsung's Vision AI Micro RGB 115-inch TV is the world's first with micro-RGB LED tech. Each tiny diode lights up red, green, and blue on its own, ditching old-school backlights for pure, vibrant colours. No more washed-out shades in dark caves or foggy battles—everything from Fortnite builds to Zelda landscapes hits with pinpoint accuracy. It's built for HDR+, boosting contrast so shadows hide secrets and highlights blind like real sunlight. For UK gamers, this means crisper views during rainy afternoons, when you're glued to the screen dodging virtual storms.

Samsung says it's part of their 2025 range, unveiled to push premium viewing forward.
