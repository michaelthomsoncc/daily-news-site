Schools Budget Support Grant Breakdown

Diving deeper, the update ties into the Schools Budget Support Grant (SBSG) for 2025 to 2026. The DfE is pumping extra funding into schools to cover overall costs. Key focus: the teacher pay award and the local government services pay award for support staff. "The Department for Education (DfE) will provide schools additional funding in financial year 2025 to 2026 through the SBSG to support them with overall costs, including the costs of the schools teacher pay award and the local government services pay award in respect of support staff in 2025 to 2026." This means schools won't have to skimp on essentials, potentially freeing up resources for tech upgrades that power your gaming clubs.
