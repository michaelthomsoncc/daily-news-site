What's Inside the Azoth 96 HE?

The ROG Azoth 96 HE rocks a 96% layout, squeezing in a numpad without hogging desk space—perfect for your UK bedroom setup where every inch counts during late-night sessions. It uses ROG HFX V2 magnetic switches with Hall effect tech, letting you adjust actuation from 0.1mm to 4.0mm. Hot-swappable means you pop out switches like LEGO bricks, no soldering mess. Tri-mode connectivity hits 2.4GHz wireless at 8000Hz polling, Bluetooth, or wired USB—zero lag for those clutch Valorant plays.

ASUS calls it "the legendary Azoth returns—now 96% with ROG HFX V2 Magnetic Switches," straight from their October 16 announcement. Gaming Features for the Win

Snap Tap mode prioritizes your last key press in FPS battles, edging out rivals in Call of Duty.
