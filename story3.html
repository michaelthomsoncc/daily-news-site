The Drop

CD Projekt RED rolled out Patch 2.1 for Cyberpunk 2077 on October 17, 2025, targeting Phantom Liberty's vehicular ecosystem. Eurogamer reports the update introduces three new Nomad-specific perks under the revamped skill tree from Patch 2.0. "These perks enhance mobility and combat integration for Nomad characters, focusing on vehicle handling and off-road prowess," states the official patch notes on the CDPR website.

Key additions include Desert Storm, which boosts weapon accuracy from moving vehicles by 25% during sandstorms—pulled straight from Dogtown's expanded weather mechanics. Then there's Pack Mule, allowing Nomads to store extra cyberware components in vehicle trunks without encumbrance penalties, easing inventory woes for long hauls. Finally, Road Rage Reload grants a 15% faster reload speed when firing from a turret-mounted weapon, verified in the patch's changelog as balancing high-speed pursuits.

This isn't fluff; it's a direct response to player feedback from the 2024 community surveys, where 62% of Nomad mains clamored for deeper vehicle synergies, per CDPR's dev diary on October 10, 2025.
