PS Plus October Catalog Drops Fresh Titles

From the Launch Lineup section – Straight facts, no filter.

PlayStation Plus subscribers in the UK and beyond just got a Halloween treat with the October 2025 Game Catalog lineup, dropping survival horror remakes and action-packed titles on October 21. Headliners: Remakes Steal the Spotlight

The catalog kicks off with the Silent Hill 2 remake, a foggy nightmare rebuilt for modern horrors, joining Extra and Premium tiers. IGN notes these arrive "just in time for Halloween," amplifying the spooky season vibe for UK gamers grinding through shorter days. No more waiting—dive straight into Pyramid Head's pursuits or mountain lodge dilemmas from your couch.

Full Lineup Packs Variety

Beyond the remakes, Alan Wake 2 headlines the action-thriller side, pulling subscribers into its meta-narrative of light versus dark in a Pacific Northwest thriller. V Rising adds vampire survival crafting, letting you build blood empires in an open world that fits right into evening sessions after work or uni. Other drops include fresh takes on classics, all verified in Sony's October 15 reveal via Gematsu. For Premium users, the Classics Catalog layers in retro gems, expanding that nostalgic pull without extra cost—ideal for UK players balancing budgets amid rising energy bills.
