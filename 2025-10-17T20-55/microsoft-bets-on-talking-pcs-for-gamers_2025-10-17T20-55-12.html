Copilot Voice Hits Windows 11

Microsoft rolled out AI upgrades to Windows 11 on Thursday, October 16, focusing on making Copilot more intuitive. The Verge reports that Copilot Voice allows users to talk to their PCs, turning standard machines into AI-driven hubs. "Microsoft is bringing new AI features to all Windows 11 PCs," the article states, emphasizing how voice commands can now handle tasks without clunky interfaces. For UK gamers juggling work and Warzone lobbies, this means dictating loadouts or querying game stats hands-free, cutting down on keyboard swaps during intense plays.
