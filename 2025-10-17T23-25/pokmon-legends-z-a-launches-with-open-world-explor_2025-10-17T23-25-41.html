Open-World Exploration in Kalos

Set entirely in Lumiose City, Legends: Z-A amps up open-world elements from Arceus. You explore rebuilt districts, customize characters, and tackle a new battle system blending action RPG with strategic fights. Wikipedia details how it focuses on urban Kalos, not vast wilds, but players praise the dense, interactive cityscape—roaming alleys for hidden Pokémon or Mega Evolution triggers. Early impressions from TechRadar highlight seamless transitions between exploration and combat, with Kalos's landmarks like Prism Tower feeling alive. UK gamers report smooth performance on Switch, no frame drops during parkour-style chases, fitting right into rainy autumn afternoons.

But some flagged the city-only setting as visually restricting compared to Arceus's open fields—still, the rebuilding mechanics keep it fresh.
