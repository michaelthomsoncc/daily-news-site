Atari's Big Intellivision Revival

It comes loaded with 45 classic games, letting you relive hits like Astrosmash and Lock 'n' Chase without dusty cartridges. Published reports highlight how this taps into the nostalgia boom, perfect for UK kids mixing old pixel art with today's high-def action.
