China-Taiwan Tensions Escalate with New Military Drills Near Strait

From the Global Alerts section – Straight facts, no filter.

The Drop

The Taiwanese Ministry of National Defense confirmed the exercises at dawn, stating in an official release: "We have detected unusual PLA activities, including live-fire zones east of Taiwan." No incursions into Taiwanese airspace yet, but radar locked on 125 sorties by midday. Beijing's Eastern Theater Command called it a "routine patrol," but coordinates overlap shipping routes carrying 50% of global container traffic—disruptions could spike PlayStation component costs overnight.
