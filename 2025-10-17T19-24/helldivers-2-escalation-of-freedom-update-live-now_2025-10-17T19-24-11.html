The Drop: What's New in the Patch

Per the official PlayStation Blog post released today, the update introduces three new stratagems: the Orbital Gatling Barrage for rapid-fire suppression, the Shield Generator Relay that deploys a mobile energy dome for team defense, and the Supply Pack upgrade allowing mid-drop resupplies without calling in a ship. Balance changes nerf the Eagle Strafing Run cooldown by 20% while buffing Illuminate shields by 15%, per patch notes. No paywall—it's free for all owners, clocking in at 2.5GB download.
