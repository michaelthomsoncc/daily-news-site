Optimal Troop Research (EI only, no Imperians): EI carry ~60 resources. For unknown background small inactive farms (pop <100), recommendations are conservative to account for possible cranny or light defense. Small counts (capped ~4-12) enable more frequent raids (5x/day rotation) instead of large ones that often return empty on defenceless villages. Data from official map.sql snapshot.
5 Lists Strategy: The 5 lists are for 5 different raid sizes (List 1 = smallest recommended EI count for the easiest tier; List 5 = largest count within these pop<100 farms). Bucket for each farm is computed from its individual population and distance (closer farms allowed into higher-count buckets because short travel times tolerate lower efficiency). Each list has one default troop amount — create 5 dedicated in-game farm lists with that fixed number, then just select the size list and add its batches. Within each list: farms kept in assignment order and chunked into batches of 10. You decide how many times to send the whole list (any size tier) per day. We still hit every target the same number of times per day because whole lists are sent at once.
